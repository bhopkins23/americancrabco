Our Story

Three generations of seafood excellence in the Delaware Valley

A Family Tradition Spanning Over 45 Years

American Crab Company is a third-generation, family-owned business with roots that run deep in the Philadelphia seafood tradition. The Green family owned the widely popular Blue Point Crab in Philadelphia, building a legendary reputation as one of the finest seafood destinations in the Delaware Valley.

Specializing in wild-caught, fresh seafood, we continue the family legacy that began over four decades ago. From a street corner in Philadelphia to our locations in Bensalem and Dublin, PA, the Green family has never wavered in our commitment to quality, freshness, and exceptional service.

Our Journey Through the Years

Late 1970s

Humble Beginnings

Fred Green Sr. starts selling crabs from a street corner in Philadelphia, wearing his signature red foam crab hat and calling out "Crabs, Crabs, Crabs...if you Want the Crabs, We've Got the Crabs!" This passionate street vendor would become the foundation of a family legacy.

1979

Blue Point Crab Opens

Fred Green Sr. purchased Blue Point Crabs in Northeast Philadelphia, officially establishing the Green family seafood business. This marked the beginning of what would become a Philadelphia institution.

1980s-90s

Building a Legend

Blue Point Crab became legendary in the Philadelphia seafood scene. The Green family built an unmatched reputation for having the best crabs in the city. Generations of Philadelphia families made Blue Point Crab their destination for fresh, quality seafood.

2000s

A New Chapter in Trevose

The Green family migrated to Trevose, PA with Fred Green Jr. taking the helm. The business was rebranded as American Crab Company on Route 1, bringing the same commitment to quality that made Blue Point Crab famous to a new location.

2010s

Expansion and Growth

Recognizing the growing demand for quality seafood, the family expanded the Trevose facility, doubling the space to better serve customers throughout the Delaware Valley. The larger location allowed for an enhanced selection and improved dining experience.

2021

Growing Our Reach

The Green family purchased Bucks County Seafood Marketplace in Dublin, PA, expanding their reach into Bucks County and allowing even more families to experience the quality and service that has defined the Green family name for decades.
