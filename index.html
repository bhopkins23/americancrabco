Our Specialties

What We're Known For

For over 45 years, customers across Bensalem, Bucks County, and the Delaware Valley have trusted us for the freshest seafood around.

Signature

Hardshell Crabs

Our steamed crabs have been our signature item and the foundation of our business. Only the finest quality, all sizes, from the best locations. Live or cooked, by the dozen or bushel.

Award-Winning

Cape May Crab Cakes

Made with only the freshest, highest-quality crabmeat. Our Cape May crab cakes have been a customer favorite for decades. You won't find a better crab cake anywhere.

Lobster Tails

Top quality cold and warm water varieties in a great selection of sizes from 4 oz and up. Cold water, warm water, Australian, and South African options available.

Wild-Caught Shrimp

Quality varieties from around the world, never farm raised, in all sizes. Fresh or frozen, steamed or raw. Always delicious.

Snow & King Crab Legs

Our snow crabs are a customer favorite—always sweet and delicious. Available by the pound or in 10 lb. bags. King crab, Dungeness, and Bairdi also available.

Fresh Fish

Wild-caught salmon, tuna, flounder, cod, halibut, swordfish, and more. Sourced from the most reputable distributors across the country, available per market conditions.

Our Story

Family Operated for Three Generations

American Crab Company is a third-generation, family-owned business dating back to 1979. The Green family owned and operated the widely popular Blue Point Crab in Philadelphia for many years before establishing our current home on Route 1 in Trevose, PA.

We specialize in selling wild-caught, fresh seafood and are proud of our reputation as one of the finest seafood destinations in the Delaware Valley. From Bensalem to Bucks County and beyond, our customers keep coming back for quality they can trust.
